Part B: Short Answer Questions (10 points)

1. Applying the anthropometric design principles, find out the dimensions of headband length of the
   earmuff hearing protection. Data is normal. Think about what design principle to use, what
   percentile(s) to use, and then calculate the appropriate dimension(s) of the headband length. (6
   points)

Headband arc length Mean (cm) SD (cm)

Female  35     5
Male
        45     3

Design principle: Design for the adjustability (5th percentile female ­ 95th percentile male)

        5th percentile female = µ - Z0.05  = 35 - (1.645 × 5) = 26.78 cm

                         95th percentile male = µ + Z0.95  = 45 + (1.645 × 3) = 49.94 cm

                                            Dimension range = (26.78, 49.94) cm
   Thus, the headband should be 49.94 cm long with an adjustable range of 23.16 cm.

Human factors implications

 Sensitivity loss:

     1. Refresh with examples of the target (pictures)

             Reduces memory load by providing an aid

     2. Training to improve sensitivity

             Automaticity can reduce workload and make it easier to distinguish between targets and
               distractors

 Response bias shift:

     1. Feedback on success / failure

             Helps people get a better estimate of the probability of noise and signals

     2. Introduce false signals

             Also works as a memory aid

     3. Allow use of confidence levels in decision

             Provide ratings of confidence rather than yes/no

Exercise 2: Time Study (4 points)

An analyst conducted a time study on a packaging operation and collected the following element times:

                                            Cycle       Element Time (sec)
                                               1                    12.3
                                               2                    11.8
                                               3                    12.5
                                               4                    12.1
                                               5                    11.9
                                               6                    12.4
                                               7                    12.0
                                               8                    11.7

The operator was rated at 105% and the allowance is 12%. Calculate the standard time for this

element.

Solution:

Step 1: Calculate average element time (ET)

ET  =     12.3+11.8+12.5+12.1+11.9+12.4+12.0+11.7   =   96.7  =    12.09  seconds
                                        8                 8

Step 2: Calculate normal time (NT)

NT  =     ET  ū  Rating  =  12.09   ū  105  =  12.09    ū  1.05    =   12.69  seconds
                   100                 100

Step 3: Calculate standard time (ST)

ST  =     NT  ū  (1  +  Allowance%  )  =  12.69  ū  (1  +  12   )  =   12.69  ū  1.12  =  14.21  seconds
                              100                          100

Answer: Standard Time = 14.21 seconds

Exercise 3: Capping a Marker (10 points)

An operator needs to pick up a marker cap from a desk 12" away (the cap is by itself) and place it onto
a marker being held in the other hand. Break down this task into its basic motions and calculate the
total time in TMU.

    My main assumption is that the hands are at the same place originally (no distance between them).
If there were distance, it would be give to us...

Motion Description                               MTM-1 Code      Time (TMU)
Reach to marker cap 12" (fixed location)               R12B             12.9
Grasp cap (small, by itself)                            G1A             2.0
Move cap 12" to marker                                 M12B             13.4
Position cap on marker (close fit, symmetrical)        P2SE             16.2
Release cap                                             RL1             2.0

                                                    Total Time:    46.5 TMU

Answer: Total time = 46.5 TMU (or 1.674 seconds, or 0.0279 minutes)

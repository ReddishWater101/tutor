## 1. Clara's Organic Lip Balm EPQ | Source: Assignments/ProblemSet_1_EOQ.pdf, Pages 2-3
Clara's is a family-owned manufacturer of organic lip balms and hand lotions. Clara's produces 5,000 lip balms a year and it costs $2 to produce a lip balm. It is estimated that the cost of setup to produce lip balms is $50, because the residue from the hand lotion production must be removed from the production line before starting the production of a lip balm batch. Inventory holding cost is based on a 12 percent annual interest rate. The demand for lip balms is 2,400 sticks per year.

a. What is the optimal batch size of a production run?

b. What is the length of a production cycle?

c. What is the average annual cost of production setup and inventory holding for lip balms?

## 2. Northwest Seafood Fresh Mussels Newsvendor | Source: Assignments/ProblemSet_2_Newsvendor.pdf, Pages 2-3
Northwest Seafood in Gainesville buys fresh mussels daily for $4.20 per two-pound bag and sells them for $5.70 per bag. At the end of each business day, any mussels remaining are sold to a cat food producer for $2.40 per bag. Daily demand for mussels can be approximated by a normal distribution with a mean of 40 bags and a standard deviation of 5 bags.

a. What is the unit cost of overage?

b. What is the unit cost of underage?

c. What is the critical ratio?

d. What is the optimal order quantity in two-pound bags?
